The Story Behind the Code

Two goals: High-performance websites and high-level soccer.

Hi, I’m the founder of LineByLine Digital. I’m 14 years old, and I believe that anything worth doing is worth doing right—whether that’s on the pitch or in a text editor.

I started this business for a very specific reason. For as long as I can remember, my life has revolved around soccer. My goal is to play at the highest level possible, competing in the NPL (National Premier Leagues). But as I’ve moved up, I’ve realized that the "pay-to-play" model is becoming a huge barrier.

⚽
The Challenge: NPL registration, travel, and elite coaching fees can cost over $2,500 - $3,000 a year. As prices keep rising, it’s getting harder for families like mine to keep up.

Why "Manual Mode"?

In soccer, you don't get better by using shortcuts. You put in the hours of training, one drill at a time. I take that same discipline to web development. While other people use automated "drag-and-drop" builders that create slow, bloated websites, I write every single line of code by hand.

I call it Manual Mode Web. It’s cleaner, it’s faster, and it’s built with the same precision I use when I’m lining up a shot.

Your Site Funds My Season

When you hire me to build your business website, you aren't just getting a fast, custom, hand-coded product for a great price ($100 for the first year). You are directly supporting a local athlete’s journey to play elite soccer.

I manage your site for $120 a year, which helps me cover my NPL registration, my boots, and my travel to matches. You get a professional online presence, and I get to keep chasing my dream on the field.
